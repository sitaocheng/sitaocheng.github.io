Short Bio

I'm currently a first-year M.Eng. student in Websoft Research Group, Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University, advised by Prof. Yuzhong Qu. 

I received my B.Sci. degree in Software College of Northeastern University, Shenyang, China in June 2020. I was advised by Prof. Guibing Guo and Associate Prof. Gang Wu.

My research interests include knowledge graph, question answering, and recommender systems.
